Advanced Urology & Nephrology Care at Ramakrishna Hospital

Ramakrishna Hospital offers comprehensive care for urological and kidney-related conditions through its dedicated Urology & Nephrology department. Our expert team of urologists and nephrologists provide state-of-the-art diagnosis, surgical intervention, and long-term disease management using advanced technology and a compassionate approach.

From kidney stones and urinary infections to dialysis and chronic kidney disease (CKD), we ensure personalized care plans and accurate treatment tailored to each patient’s condition and lifestyle needs.

“With cutting-edge technology and compassionate care, we’re here to support your urinary and kidney health — every step of the way.”

Our Key Services Include

Our specialists treat a wide range of urological and nephrological disorders with precision and care.

  • Kidney Stone Treatment & Lithotripsy
  • Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Management
  • Prostate & Bladder Disorders
  • Dialysis Services & Kidney Failure Care
  • Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) Management

At Ramakrishna Hospital, we combine clinical excellence with advanced diagnostic tools to deliver the best outcomes in urological and nephrological care. Our integrated approach ensures early detection, effective treatment, and continuous support for long-term health.

We strive to create a welcoming environment where patients feel valued respected and well informed about their health
