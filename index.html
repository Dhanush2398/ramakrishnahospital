Our Facilities

Trauma & Emergency Services

Our fully equipped emergency unit provides immediate care for trauma, accidents, and critical health issues—available 24/7 with expert staff.

Medical ICU

Our Medical ICU offers critical care for patients with severe health conditions, monitored continuously by specialized intensivists and staff.

Surgical ICU

Equipped with modern technology, our Surgical ICU handles post-operative care with round-the-clock monitoring and infection control protocols.

Endoscopy

Our advanced endoscopy unit offers minimally invasive diagnostics and treatment for gastrointestinal conditions using high-definition imaging.

Colonoscopy

We provide safe and accurate colonoscopy procedures to detect and manage colorectal issues, ensuring early diagnosis and treatment.

ECG & Digital X Ray

Physiotherapy

Laboratory Services

Our NABL-accredited laboratory ensures precise diagnostics with quick turnaround, covering hematology, pathology, and biochemistry tests.

Pharma & Ambulance

Your Questions About Ramakrishna Hospital – Answered

At Ramakrishna Hospital, we’re committed to clear, transparent communication with our patients. Here are answers to some of the most common questions about our services, facilities, and appointments.

1.How can I book an appointment with a specialist?

You can book an appointment through our official website, by calling our front desk, or by walking into our reception. We also offer appointment scheduling via WhatsApp for select departments.

2.Where can I get a copy of my medical reports?

Medical reports can be collected from the records section at the hospital. Alternatively, patients can also request a digital copy via email or through our patient portal, if registered.

3.Do you offer 24/7 emergency services?

Yes, Ramakrishna Hospital provides 24/7 emergency services with fully equipped trauma care and on-call specialists available around the clock.

4.What insurance providers are accepted?

We accept a wide range of insurance providers and government health schemes. Please contact our billing department for the most up-to-date list or visit the insurance help desk on-site.

5.Is there a pharmacy and diagnostic lab on the premises?

Yes, we have a 24-hour in-house pharmacy and a fully functional diagnostic lab offering blood tests, X-rays, ECGs, MRIs, and more—all under one roof.
